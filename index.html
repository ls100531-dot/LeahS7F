How to write a book in 8 phases

The first 7 phases of writing a book are the most important because planning is key to a perfect book . The first phase is brain storming which helps build the foundation of your boook .
The second phase is create your characters . The thrid phase is to plan were and what its about . The forth phase choose locations and names of places.
The fifth phase is choosing what happens to all the characters in the end . The sixth phase is to write your first draft of the book . The seventh phase is to finalise your last copy of the book .
The eighth phase is to plan and draw your front cover .
